    Early Life

    Christopher Nicholas Sarantakos, popularly known by his stage name of Criss Angel, was born in Hempstead, New York, on December 19, 1967, to his parents Dimitra Sarantakos and John Sarantakos. He was interested in magic when he was seven years old.

    He completed his schooling at East Meadow High School. Following that, he went on to pursue a career in magic instead of going to college.

    Personal Life

    In 2002, Criss Angel got married to JoAnn Winkhart, after dating each other for a long time. The couple divorced four years after their marriage. In 2008, he was in a relationship with Holly Madison. They broke up in early 2009. In 2011, he got engaged to Sandra Gonzalez.

    Criss is also a philanthropist and is the founder of Believe Foundation ‘Believe Anything is Possible.’

    In 1997, Criss Angel appeared in the made-for-television movie ‘The Science of Magic.’ He has also appeared in its sequel. His first television series was ‘Criss Angel Mindfreak.’ It aired from 2005 to 2010. In 2007, he became the judge of the television show ‘Phenomenon.’
