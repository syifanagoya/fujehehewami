    November may be right around the corner, but that is not stopping Kaia Gerber from wearing her summer threads well into fall (this is what happens when you reside in Los Angeles). On Tuesday, the model gave us a break from Halloween content with a selfie of her wearing a sheer gray tube top and white pants, making us nostalgic for warmer temps.

    In the image shared to her Instagram, Gerber ran both hands through her hair showing off her sleek Celine by Hedi Slimane Cuir Triomphe Heloise bag, which she paired with a matching cropped tube top. On the bottom, she wore stark white low-rise slacks, breaking that one silly fashion rule that no one follows anyway. Her shoulder-length lob was gentle tousled and parted down the middle, and her no-makeup glam look included dewy skin that glistened in the beaming sunlight.
