    Early Life

    Born on July 27, 1969, in Nashua, New Hampshire, Paul saw his first wrestling match at the age of five and started harboring ambitions to become a bodybuilder at the age of fourteen.

    He went to Nashua South High School and after graduating, entered bodybuilding competitions and was eventually introduced to the wrestling world by Ted Arcidi.

    He had begun an onscreen romance with Stephanie McMahon in 2000. Soon, this blossomed into real-time romance, and they got married in 2003. They now have three daughters together.

    He also released his own book titled, Making the Game: Triple H’s Approach to a Better Body in 2004.

    Age, Height, and Weight

    Being born on July 27, 1969, Triple H is 54 years old as of today’s date 20th July 2024. His height is 1.93 m tall, and his weight is 116 kg.

    Career

    His first professional foray into wrestling was in the International Wrestling Federation (IWF).

    In 1994, he signed a one year deal with World Championship Wrestling (WCW). His ring name at the time was modified to Terra Ryzing.

    He left the WCW for WWF in 1995.

    He made his WWF debut in SummerSlam, where he defeated Bob Holly.

    In 1996, Triple H appeared in a match against Marty Garner in one of the episodes on Superstar.

    He was also rumored to be a part of The Kliq that is said to have influenced the creative Team of WWF.

    From 1997, he became a part of D-Generation X (DX). Their promos were extremely provocative in nature. At the time, he had a feud with the Hart family, and it culminated in a showdown in WrestleMania.

    After WrestleMania, he took leadership of the DX and also formed a rivalry with The Rock.

    He won his first WWF championship by defeating Mankind.

    In 2001, he and Steve Austin formed a two-man power team.

    He defeated Chris Jericho is WrestleMania18 to win the WWE title.

    In 2003, he became the leader of a group called Evolution.

    In 2006-07 the DX was reformed, which made Triple H one of the fan favorites once again.

    He appeared in RAW, SmackDown, SummerSlam and Royal Rumble at various times and defeated different opponents.

    In 2011, he assumed the position of the Chief Operating Officer (COO) of the WWE.

    He also became the leader of The Authority.

    Awards and Achievements

    In 1999, he defeated Mankind for his first WWE Championship Award.

    Triple H emerged victorious in the Six-Pack Challenge in 1999 to win his second WWE Championship.

    He secured the championship again after defeating Big Show in 2000.

    His fourth win was against The Rock in the same year.

    At WrestleMania, X8, he defeated Chris Jericho to secure another win.

    He became the World Heavyweight Champion in 2002.

    Triple H secured the World Heavyweight Championship in The Three Stages of Hell in 2002 and held the title for nine months.

    In the Elimination chamber of 2009, he defeated all the big names to earn the most WWE Championships held by a single Superstar.
